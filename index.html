Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 5, 2025

Privacy Summary: CleanPrompt.ai does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data. Everything happens locally in your browser.

1. Introduction

CleanPrompt.ai ("we," "our," or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices for the CleanPrompt.ai browser extension.

Developer: Aron Innovations
Website: https://aroninnovations.com

2. Data Collection

2.1 No Data Collection

CleanPrompt.ai does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or usage data. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your ChatGPT conversations
  • We do not collect your browsing history
  • We do not collect identifiable information (name, email, IP address, etc.)
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies
  • We do not use analytics or telemetry services
  • We do not transmit any data to external servers

2.2 Local Operation Only

The extension operates entirely within your browser. All processing happens locally on your device. No information ever leaves your computer.

3. How the Extension Works

CleanPrompt.ai works by interacting with ChatGPT pages in your browser to keep conversations clean and on-topic. This happens entirely on your device:

  • The extension only activates on chat.openai.com and chatgpt.com
  • It operates in real-time as you use ChatGPT
  • All behavior is contained within your browser tab
  • No data is sent to our servers (we don't have servers)

4. Permissions

4.1 Site Access

The extension requests access to:

  • https://chat.openai.com/*
  • https://chatgpt.com/*

Why: This permission is required for the extension to function on ChatGPT pages and provide clean, on-topic conversations. The extension does not affect any other websites.

4.2 No Additional Permissions

CleanPrompt.ai does not request any additional Chrome permissions such as:

  • Storage (we don't store data)
  • Network access (we don't make external requests)
  • Clipboard access
  • Download permissions

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 No Third-Party Services

CleanPrompt.ai does not integrate with, connect to, or share data with any third-party services, including:

  • Analytics platforms (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media platforms
  • Cloud storage services
  • External APIs

5.2 OpenAI/ChatGPT

While CleanPrompt.ai operates on ChatGPT pages, it does not share data with OpenAI beyond what you already share by using ChatGPT directly. Your use of ChatGPT is subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy, not ours.

6. Data Security

Since CleanPrompt.ai does not collect, store, or transmit any data, there is no user data that could be compromised. The extension's code runs entirely in your browser with no external communication.

7. Children's Privacy

CleanPrompt.ai does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. Since we collect no data at all, the extension can be used by individuals of any age in compliance with applicable laws.

8. International Users

CleanPrompt.ai operates locally in your browser regardless of your location. Since no data is collected or transmitted, there are no cross-border data transfer concerns.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Significant changes will be communicated through:

  • Extension update notes in the Chrome Web Store
  • Our website at aroninnovations.com

10. Your Rights

Since CleanPrompt.ai does not collect any personal data, data rights such as access, correction, deletion, and portability do not apply. You maintain complete control over your browsing experience:

  • Uninstall: You can remove the extension at any time via chrome://extensions
  • Disable: You can temporarily disable the extension without uninstalling
  • Transparency: The extension's behavior is limited to ChatGPT pages only
